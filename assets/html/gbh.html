ABOUT MY INTERNSHIP

It was exciting to intern at a company so entrenched with my early childhood.

GBH (formerly known as WGBH) is the main PBS member television station licensed to Boston, Massachusetts, and is responsible for many notable programs like Arthur, Curious George, Masterpiece, and NOVA. As someone who grew up watching many of the company’s shows, it was really moving to work directly at the source.

SOME TAKEAWAYS

My responsibilities consisted of redesigning GBH’s main website, conducting remote interviews and A/B tests on target users, and producing creative assets for online games related to the children’s show Arthur.

Please reach out via email for more detailed case studies.
