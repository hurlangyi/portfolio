ABOUT MY INTERNSHIP

I’m grateful to have worked with an amazing team of designers during an unprecedented summer.

Working remotely from home definitely posed a lot of challenges, especially when onboarding onto a new team and collaborating with others over research and design. However, everyone on the Core Product Design Team was always open to help, offer advice on navigating this uncertain time, and show me how to have fun together.

My responsibilities consisted of UX research and interaction design work. I was involved with Visa’s installment payment options. Half of my work consisted of remote user research and presenting my findings to adjacent product managers; the other half focused on redesigning interface designs based on user feedback.


Please reach out to learn more about this internship.

SOME TAKEAWAYS

UX Language is incredibly important.

It’s integral that we don’t confuse users with unknown terminology and don’t mislead them with anything we present.

Listen to your users!

Different users have different ideas about the products you make - balancing all these diverse opinions will help you decide what’s desirable vs. what’s feasible.

Expand beyond design.

Communication with other non-design team members and discussing their insights is important. And don’t be afraid to reach out to others, even in a remote environment!
