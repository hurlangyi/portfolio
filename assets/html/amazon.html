ABOUT MY INTERNSHIP

I spent Summer 2021 as a UX Designer Intern on the Amazon F3 team, conducting research on Whole Foods Market and Amazon Fresh and creating platforms that make it easier for customers to provide feedback on their grocery shopping.

My 12 weeks at Amazon have been one of the most enriching experiences I’ve had so far—the work I did involved several logistical and time-sensitive challenges that I was trusted to tackle.

It was also my first time operating within a massive ecosystem like Amazon. I learned a lot about collaborating across different time zones, operating within a sprawling design network, and committing to my decisions. My project helped push me into becoming a better, more confident designer.

Please reach out to view my case study from this internship.

SOME TAKEAWAYS

Be confident!

Take pride in the designs you produce and back up your decisions with solid research and evidence.

Stay empathetic.

Always push to learn and empathize with your users’ needs and understand how they relate to the problem at hand.

Learn, learn, learn.

Stay open and absorb as much as you can about your problem domain and company! Treat every moment as a learning opportunity.
